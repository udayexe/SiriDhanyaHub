# Siri Dhanya Hub
### Empowering Karnataka's Millet Value Chain

Siri Dhanya Hub is more than just an app; it’s a digital ecosystem built to support the "Millet Revolution" in Karnataka. Known as the "forgotten grains," millets are climate-resilient and highly nutritious. This app is designed to help farmers get better prices and help consumers lead healthier lives.

## 🌟 Key Features
• **📈 Mandi Watch:** Stay updated with live market prices for various millets (Pearl, Barnyard, Finger Millet, etc.) to help farmers make informed selling decisions.
• **🍱 Recipe Lab:** A digital cookbook featuring traditional and modern Kannada recipes to bring millets back into our daily diet.
• **💚 Health Benefits:** Detailed nutritional breakdowns and health facts to educate users on why millets are a "Superfood."
• **🤝 Buy Direct:** A dedicated portal to connect consumers directly with local FPOs (Farmer Producer Organizations), ensuring fair trade and fresh produce.

## 🛡️ Secure & Private
We value the privacy of our users. The app features:
• **Secure Authentication:** A 4-digit PIN lock to keep your data safe.
• **Biometric Access:** Login quickly and securely using Fingerprint or Face ID.
• **Encrypted Storage:** All sensitive data is stored using industry-standard EncryptedSharedPreferences with hardware-level security.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
• **Language:** Kotlin
• **Architecture:** MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) with Clean Architecture principles.
• **UI:** Material 3 Design Components for a natural, earthy user experience.
• **DI:** Hilt (Dagger) for dependency injection.
• **Jetpack:** Navigation Component, ViewBinding, and Lifecycle.
• **Security:** Android Security Crypto library & Biometric API.

### Why I built this:
Millets need 70% less water than rice and are vital for a sustainable future. I built Siri Dhanya Hub to support Karnataka's farmers and make this healthy alternative accessible to everyone through a modern, secure, and easy-to-use mobile experience.
